System Vision

A collaborative workflow and project management SaaS that enables teams to create projects, manage tasks, assign work, and track execution through structured workflows and role-based access control in a multi-tenant environment.

Target Environment

Web-based SaaS (multi-tenant, cloud-deployed)

Core Objectives
Project & task lifecycle management
Team collaboration inside workspaces
Role-based access control
Workflow tracking (Kanban system)
Scalable SaaS architecture
